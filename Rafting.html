River Rafting in Rishikesh

Experience the thrill of white-water rafting in the holy Ganges. Choose from a variety of rafting distances and difficulty levels.

12km Rafting

Duration: 2-3 hours

Difficulty: Easy to Moderate

Highlights: Shivpuri to Lakshman Jhula, small rapids, scenic views.

₹700/- per person

16km Rafting

Duration: 3-4 hours

Difficulty: Moderate

Highlights: Marine Drive to Lakshman Jhula, thrilling rapids, lush greenery.

₹1000/- per person

26km Rafting

Duration: 4-5 hours

Difficulty: Moderate to Challenging

Highlights: Kaudiyala to Lakshman Jhula, long stretches, intense rapids.

₹1500/- per person

32km Rafting

Duration: 5-6 hours

Difficulty: Challenging

Highlights: Brahmpuri to Rishikesh, longest stretch, extreme rapids.

₹2500/- per person
