Explore Rishikesh - The Yoga Capital of the World

About Rishikesh

Rishikesh, nestled in the foothills of the Himalayas along the banks of the sacred Ganges River, is a serene town known for its spiritual significance and adventure activities. It is a hub for yoga and meditation enthusiasts, offering numerous ashrams and wellness centers. Rishikesh is also a gateway to the Himalayas and the starting point for the Char Dham Yatra.
